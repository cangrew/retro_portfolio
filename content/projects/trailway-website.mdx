Marketing website for Trailway, a professional dispatching service for truckers and freight carriers. Single-page design with smooth scrolling, a contact form backed by Resend transactional email, and deployment on Vercel.

Built with Next.js 16 (App Router), React 19, Tailwind CSS v4, and TypeScript.
